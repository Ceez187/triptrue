💡 Split Fare Rules
Two separate one-way tickets — airlines don't know they're paired
Build in buffer time between legs — a delay on leg 1 won't protect you on leg 2
Southwest: 2 free checked bags always. Allegiant/Spirit/Frontier: bags cost more sometimes
Best splits happen when one airline has a sale on one direction only
